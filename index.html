Our Process

A Straightforward Path to Ownership

Four steps. Full transparency. No pressure.

01

Discovery

Goals, budget, and timeline. A shortlist within 24 hours.

We begin with a private consultation to understand exactly what you're looking for — whether it's an investment property, a family home, or a Golden Visa-eligible unit. No pressure, just clarity.

02

Selection

Vetted options with payment plans, yields, and location context.

We present a curated shortlist with transparent data — rental yields, capital appreciation trends, developer track records, and payment plan comparisons side by side.

03

Viewing

Virtual or on-site tours with clear side-by-side comparison.

Whether you're in Dubai or abroad, we arrange immersive virtual tours or in-person visits. Every property is presented with honest context — the strengths and the trade-offs.

04

Completion

Contracts, DLD registration, escrow, and handover handled.

From SPA review to Dubai Land Department registration, escrow coordination, and final handover — we manage every document and deadline so nothing falls through the cracks.

05

Ongoing Support

Leasing, management, and portfolio growth — long after handover.

Your relationship with Alexandria doesn't end at the handover. We offer property management, tenant placement, resale advisory, and ongoing portfolio reviews as your investment grows.
